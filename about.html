Our Story

Printing your memories since day one

Panchsheel Customizers Sublimation — known locally as Panchsheel Custom Lab — began as a small printing counter in Nandgram, Ghaziabad. One heat press. One promise: every print must feel personal.

Today, under the hands of proprietor Sanjay Kumar, our studio prints everything from a single anniversary mug to 500-piece school uniform orders. What hasn't changed is the promise — every print, every time, done right.

We're not a faceless online store. We're your local print shop — we'll open the package with you, match the shade with you, and reprint for free if anything is off. That's the Panchsheel way.
