FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you deliver my order? +

Most single-piece orders (mug, t-shirt, frame) are ready the same day if ordered before 12pm. Bulk orders of 50+ pieces usually take 1–3 working days.

What's the minimum order quantity? +

Just 1 piece. One mug for a birthday or 500 uniforms for an event — we do both with the same care.

I don't have a design, can you create one for me? +

Yes. Our in-house design team creates professional artwork free of cost on orders above ₹500. Share your idea on WhatsApp — we'll send 2 preview options before printing.

Do you deliver outside Ghaziabad? +

Yes. Free pickup in Nandgram, doorstep delivery across Delhi NCR, and courier shipping pan-India. Shipping charges confirmed on WhatsApp.

Will the print fade after washing? +

No. We use industrial sublimation & DTF printing with imported inks — it's rated for the lifetime of the fabric. If you see fading within 6 months, we reprint free.

Do you accept UPI / card payments? +

Yes — UPI, PhonePe, GPay, Paytm, cash and bank transfer all accepted. 30% advance on bulk orders, balance on delivery.
